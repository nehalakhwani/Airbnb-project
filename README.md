About this Project
Analysed 50,000 rows using Python, Power Query and Power BI to evaluate Airbnb market performance across cities using data-driven insights.
Key Insights
•	Airbnb listings peaked in 2019, indicating the highest market activity during the observed period.
•	With 30% availability, it looks like demand is quite High.
•	Listings are distributed across cities, with a few major cities contributing the highest share, as shown by the cumulative percentage.
•	Entire homes cost about twice as much as private rooms.
•	Private rooms have the highest share of reviews at 37%.
•	Staten Island has the highest number of listings, while Woodside has the lowest.
•	Total revenue reached around 7 million, showing strong overall performance.
Tools Used
Power Query: Data Transformation
Python: identified missing values and replaced them using mean, median, or mode.
Power BI: Data Visualisation
Recommendations
•	Focus more on high-demand cities to increase bookings and revenue.
•	Use Discount strategies to low- demand cities to attract new customers.
•	Offering occasional discounts can help increase occupancy when demand is low.
•	Promote private rooms as they receive the highest reviews.
•	Prioritising entire homes can be beneficial due to their higher pricing potential.
•	Adjust pricing for low-performing listings to make them more competitive.
•	Overall, using data insights regularly can help in making better decisions and improving performance.
These recommendations help improve revenue performance, optimise pricing strategies, and identify high-potential locations for better market growth and efficiency.
